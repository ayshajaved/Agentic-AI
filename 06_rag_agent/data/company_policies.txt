[WORK FROM HOME POLICY]
- Employees can work from home twice a week.
- Request must be submitted via the portal 48 hours in advance.
- Internet stipend is $50 per month.

[LEAVE POLICY]
- 20 days of paid annual leave.
- Unlimited sick leave with a doctor's note for more than 3 days.
- Paternity leave is 4 weeks. Maternity leave is 16 weeks.

[TRAVEL REIMBURSEMENT]
- Economy class for flights under 6 hours.
- Business class for flights over 6 hours.
- Daily food allowance during travel is $75.

[OFFICE HOURS]
- Core hours are 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM.
- Total expected work hours are 40 hours per week.
